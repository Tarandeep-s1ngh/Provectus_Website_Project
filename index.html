Products

We deal in various classes of medicines from Analgesics to Multivitamins. All our products are trademark registered with Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India.

Analgesics

Analgesics are medicines that are used to relieve pain. They are also known as painkillers or pain relievers

Antibiotics

Antibiotics are medicines that help stop infections caused by bacteria

Antidepressants

Antidepressants are used to treat major depressive disorder, some anxiety disorders etc

Antifungal

An antifungal medication is a pharmaceutical fungicide or fungistatic used to treat and prevent mycosis such as athlete's foot, ringworm, candidiasis (thrush), and others

Antacids

Antacids are a class of medicines that neutralize stomach acidity and is used to relieve heartburn, indigestion or an upset stomach

multivitamins

Multivitamins are used to treat or prevent vitamin deficiency due to poor diet or certain illnesses with vitamins, dietary minerals, and other nutritional elements
